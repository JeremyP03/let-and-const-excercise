What is the difference between var and let?
A. you cannot redeclare with let.

What is the deference between var and const?
A. you cannot redeclare nor reassing with const but you can do both of them with var.

What is the difference between let and const?
A. you cannot redeclare with let but you can reassing with let, with const you cannot reassing nor redeclare.

What is hoisting?
A. you can access the variable before it being defined but it will be undefined.
